Welcome to the VCCC!

Hey collectors, it's Harold. After 34 years of hunting down obscure belt-driven uprights at estate sales, I finally got a server running so we can properly catalogue what we've got. Whether you're chasing pre-war Electrolux canisters, a mint-condition Kirby Sanitronic, or just got your first Hoover 700 — you belong here. Log in, track your hauls, and connect with fellow suction enthusiasts worldwide.

To get started, create a member account and choose one of the 3 Collection Tracks:

  • Upright Track    (Domestic Powerhouses, 1910s–1980s)
  • Canister Track   (European Elegance & the Golden Age)
  • Robovac Track    (Autonomous Units & Modern Curiosities)
