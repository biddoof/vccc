Annual Convention

The VCCC Annual Convention is the club's flagship event, held each year in a different host city. It brings together collectors, restorers, and historians for a weekend of displays, talks, trades, and camaraderie.

The convention typically includes a show floor where members display their collections, a swap meet for buying, selling, and trading machines and parts, and presentations from collectors on topics ranging from early electric models to restoration techniques.

Past Conventions

The VCCC has held annual conventions since the club's early years. Past events have been hosted across the United States, with attendance ranging from dedicated local members to collectors traveling internationally.

If you have photos, programs, or records from past conventions you'd like to contribute to the club archives, please get in touch.
