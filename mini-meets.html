Mini Meets

Mini meets are smaller, informal regional gatherings organized by VCCC members throughout the year. They provide an opportunity to meet local collectors, see machines in person, swap parts, and share knowledge without the travel required for the annual convention.

Mini meets are typically hosted by a member at their home, a rented hall, or another suitable venue. Attendance varies from a handful of collectors to a few dozen depending on location and interest.

Upcoming Mini Meets

No mini meets are currently scheduled. Upcoming events will be listed here and announced in the club newsletter.

If you'd like to host a mini meet in your area, please contact us — we can help spread the word to members in your region.

Host a Mini Meet

Hosting a mini meet is a great way to grow the local collector community. A typical mini meet just needs a space large enough to display a few machines and room for collectors to gather and talk. The club can provide guidance and help announce the event to nearby members.

Interested? Get in touch with us and we'll help you get started.
