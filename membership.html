Membership

VCCC membership is open to anyone with an interest in vacuum cleaner history, collecting, or restoration — from seasoned collectors with rooms full of machines to those who just picked up their first vintage Hoover.

Members receive access to the club newsletter, the annual convention, regional mini meets, and a community of knowledgeable collectors willing to help identify, date, and restore machines.

Membership Dues

Membership TypeAnnual Dues
Individual (U.S.)$20
Family (U.S.)$25
Individual (International)$30
Family (International)$35

Membership runs January 1 through December 31.

★ How to Join ★

To become a member, send your name, mailing address, and email address along with your dues payment (check payable to VCCC) to our membership coordinator. See the Contact Us page for the current mailing address.

Members will receive a welcome packet and the most recent club newsletter by mail.

Member Benefits

  • Quarterly club newsletter with collecting tips, restoration guides, and member finds
  • Access to the annual convention and swap meet
  • Invitations to regional mini meets
  • Listing in the member directory (optional)
  • Access to the club's research archives and identification resources
  • Discounts on convention registration for current members
